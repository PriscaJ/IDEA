Roles and Responsibilities

Are you ready to start a venture? The first step in registering your venture with IDEA is to attend a New Venture Orientation. You must have some connection with Northeastern University to take advantage of our program, such as being a student, an alumni, or a faculty member. The New Venture Orientation meetings take place every other Tuesday at 6pm in the IDEA lab. This is a mandatory meeting to start with IDEA as we go more in-depth about our three stage-gate process and explain the important milestones to achieve in each. Sign-up below to get started!
