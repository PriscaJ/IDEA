About Us

IDEA began in 2009 with strong student leadership and university support, the program was developed to provide resources and support for entrepreneurs across all disciplines.

Our unique stage gate process provides a framework that can be applied to any venture across all industries.

Today, IDEA continues to develop and expand in order to meet the needs of ventures and the entrepreneurs beind them.
