Become a Coach

Roles and Responsibilities

Another way to get involved with IDEA is by becoming a Coach. The ideal candidate for a Coach is someone who has some type of industry expertise.

There are two ways to get involved as a Coach: either as a Venture Coach or as a Ready Stage Coach. For Ready Stage Coaches, the expertise needed is more about product or service validation; for Venture Coaches, itâ€™s all about the business model development.

Ventures Coaches have the opportunity to work with ventures that align more with their area of expertise and will have a one on one relationship with a venture. You are expected to check in regularly, and should have an ongoing, working relationship with your venture. As a Venture Coach, you will have the opportunity to dive deep into helping entrepreneurs solve problems about their business model.

Ready stage coaches will not have a designated venture, but rather they will work with any and all ventures that need insight mostly through weekly coaching sessions. Ventures going through the program will sign-up for office hours and you will be there to help answer questions.
